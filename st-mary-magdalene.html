About the Series

A Space for
Live Music

The St Mary Magdalene Concert Series is a programme of lunchtime concerts held at St Mary Magdalene Church. Curated by Ramon Branch Biescas, the series is open to performers of all disciplines and provides an intimate and welcoming setting for live music in the heart of the community.

Each concert takes place at 1:00 PM and is free to attend. Performers are invited to propose their own programme — from solo recitals to chamber ensembles, early music to contemporary works.

If you would like to propose a concert, please fill in the submission form below. All proposals are considered and respondents will be contacted within two weeks.

The Vision

Why This
Series Matters

Live music has the power to transform a lunchtime into something memorable. The series exists to give performers a real platform and audiences a reason to pause — a quiet moment of beauty in an otherwise busy day.

St Mary Magdalene Church offers exceptional acoustics and a welcoming atmosphere. Whether you are an emerging musician looking for performance opportunities or an established artist wishing to reach a new audience, this series provides a meaningful context for your work.

The series is run on the belief that great music should be accessible to everyone — which is why all concerts are free.
