St Mary Magdalene
Concert Series

A curated programme of lunchtime concerts at St Mary Magdalene Church, London. Open to performers of all disciplines — classical, jazz, world music, and contemporary.

About the Series

A Venue for
Emerging & Established
Artists

St Mary Magdalene Church provides an intimate, acoustically beautiful setting for chamber music, solo recitals, and experimental performances. The series celebrates artistic diversity and creates opportunities for musicians at all career stages.

Why Perform Here?

St Mary Magdalene offers a unique opportunity to reach an engaged, cultured audience in one of London's most beautiful historic churches. The intimate venue creates a direct connection between performer and listener.

Whether you're a classical ensemble, jazz musician, singer, or experimental artist, we welcome proposals that bring fresh perspectives to our concert series.

Intimate Acoustics

Beautiful natural acoustics in a 300-seat historic church

Engaged Audience

Cultured, attentive listeners who appreciate diverse musical genres

Professional Support

Technical assistance, promotion, and artist support included

Flexible Programming

Classical, jazz, world music, contemporary — all welcome
